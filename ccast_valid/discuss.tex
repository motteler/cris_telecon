\frametitle{Discussion II}
\begin{itemize}

  \item The apodized differences between UW and UMBC A4 are very
    small, for all three bands.  Given the significant differences in
    the processing algorithms, this is reassuring.

  \item The low frequency differences of NOAA vs UMBC and UW are
    probably due to NOAA having the polarization correction, while the
    high frequency differences of UMBC vs NOAA and UW are probably due
    to the different calibration algorithms.

  \item In validation tests, UMBC used reference truth with a sinc
    basis, while UW and NOAA used reference truth convolved with
    instrument responsivity, to get similar residuals.

  \item The difference in reference truth can make a significant
    difference in the calibrated products; see references and the
    example in the appendix.

\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
